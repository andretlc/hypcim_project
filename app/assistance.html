Simply get in touch with our technical customer services team. Our Support team will use Cim HelpLogin to create a secure connection to share temporary control of your device. They can then check what the problem is – and fix it remotely if possible. Or if you’re just not sure how to do something, our Support team can show you how – on your own device.
We do our billing online, and you'll need to register for My CIM to see it in full. Once you've registered with My CIM, you'll receive an email to let you know that your bill's ready. When you log in, you can view you bill online or as a PDF that you can save or print whenever you like. If you haven't registered for My CIM, we'll send you a text with your total bill amount instead. We don't send paper bills unless you specifically ask us to. There's a monthly charge of £1.54 a bill for this service. To opt in to paper bills, call support team.
